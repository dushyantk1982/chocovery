Our Product USP – Imagination to Reality

WPC

Wood-plastic composite (WPC) is a very promising and sustainable green material that is produced for its ability to be durable without containing toxic chemicals. The physical and mechanical properties of WPC depend greatly on the interaction of the plant and polymer fibers within the material. The Wood-plastic Composites manufactured by us are produced with highly durable raw material to ensure product longevity. The Wooden-plastic composites manufactured by us are termite proof and water proof and have longer life span. The products that are fabricated using these composites are attractive in design and durable in structure. People more and more opting for the walls and wooden flooring made up of WPC material.

WHAT IS WPC?

  • The eco-friendly greener substitute for hardwood
  • Magic of steel like wood, extreme outdoor longevity
  • Water proof, termite proof, fungal proof, moth proof
  • Rot resistant, chemical & UV resistant, Fire retardant
  • Highly durable, less maintenance & high strength
  • 100% recycled, sustains temperature of 40 to 60 degrees
  • Can remain unpainted & can be painted to choice
  • Easy to install, excellent screwing, carving, cutting

WPC- WORLD OF ENDLESS POSSIBILITIES

Wood Polymer Composites have endless possibilities with comparison to conventional ply boards, MDF and natural wood. It can be used to make versatile furniture articles, wardrobes, kitchen panels, doors, and poolside decking and immense creative articles of use.

Just try, use and explore the world of endless possibilities with wood polymer composites, the best gift to save our nature and keep our planet much cleaner and greener.
