7th MP Exportech 2015 at Gwalior

Madhya Pradesh is a state brimming with amazing resources, terrific potential and tremendous business opportunities worth exploring and finding their logical end in meeting global demands.

 

The 7th MP EXPORTECH at Gwalior, which is a joint collaboration of Madhya Pradesh Laghu Udyog Nigam Ltd. along with Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) offered investors, buyers, entrepreneurs and experts a unique platform to explore bigger, better and more beneficial trade possibilities. The event was for 3 days for 16, 17 & 18th January at the Export Facilitation Center of Gwalior Trade Fair premises.
