Digi – Stone Dices

Through out the manufacturing process to finishing we focused our quality rather than quantity. As we have developed a whole unique technique to get finish the most popular stone among the Eastern, Western, European countries.

As we have customized it as per the use and application. We have solved the most common problem about its application in interior and exterior and we have the option to customized it to any size or color or graphics given.

Now customers are free to provide us the respective required image and sizes and we can give exactly what they requires.

Again, it is a natural stone which is machines as per required.

Specifications :

  • Customizable thickness .
  • Length and Breadth can be customized to any odd size(irrespective of standard sizes).

Finishing Process :

Firstly a special quality of fine grade stone is CHEMICALLY treated, then pre-coating done before DIGITAL FINISHING process and at last a post-coat is applied, then we get the final -  A GRADE finished product.
