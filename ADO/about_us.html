About Us

Introduction

Yashvi Agro Industries is established with an aim to provide best of the services at effective cost without compromising the QUALITY of the product, in Interior and Exterior Industry.

At Ado Surface, we employs latest technology with a sophisticated team of professionals and in house adaptable technology and features spread over an area of 30000 sq ft.

We are the prominent manufacturer, trader and supplier of our qualitative Digital Series products comprises of – Digital Doors, Digi-Glass, Digital WPC products, ACP panels, MDF, Tiles, Ply Board, Acrylic Sheets, Metal Sheet, and Development of other products is still on.

FAQ about ADO Surface production

1. What kind of stickers you use?
There are no stickers! Images are printed DIRECTLY to the surface, which gives you completely different finish – there are no bubbles, or loosed ends, or uneven edges.

2. What is the lifetime of the coating?
“ADO Surface” ™ Inks has 8+ point light fastness rating, which is the highest according to international scale. Which means it is anti fading and has a long lasting life.

3. If your chemical smells?
Ecological compatibility of our inks (not chemical) is confirmed by hygienic certificate. Its completely smell free. Moreover due to special silver Nano particles printed image has anti-microbes ability.
